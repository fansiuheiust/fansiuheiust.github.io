Unnamed Dungeon Game

Learn more
This game is inspired by Hypixel Skyblock's dungeons (Catacombs), made for my secondary school's open day.
In this game, the dungeon is procedurally generated in a square grid filled with rooms. The rooms include: combat room, puzzle room, blacksmith room, mini-boss room, and a pre-boss room.
Spawned in a starting room, the player will explore the dungeon to fight, obtain gears, gain buffs, and so on, where clearing a room will unlock adjacent rooms.
Should the player reach the pre-boss room, they can initiate a final boss fight.
